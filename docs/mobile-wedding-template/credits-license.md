## Reuse Guidance

You can use this adapted template as a starting point for a personal wedding invitation or a customized client invitation.

Do:

- replace the sample names, copy, venue, and images
- keep credit to Codrops/Yuriy Artyukh for the original effect
- check dependency licenses for your use case
- test on the final deployed URL before sharing

Do not:

- sell the upstream Codrops demo as-is
- redistribute the original resource as an unchanged package
- remove required third-party credits from a derivative that still uses the original effect
- ship sample couple details or placeholder images as if they were your own final content

## Image Rights

Replace the sample images with images you have the right to use.

For real wedding invitations:

- use your own photos
- confirm photographer usage rights
- avoid exposing private guest/contact details in public repositories
- consider keeping private account numbers or direct phone numbers out of public demo copies
